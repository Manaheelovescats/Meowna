    • Dos and Donts

    There are some things we must not do as cat owners to our cats,
    and there are things we must do to them.

    A Few of Your Cats' Likings

    A list of some of the things we should do that cats like.

    • Do play with them if you can. Doing this helps your pet exercise and makes them less stressed.
    • Do feed them wet cat food. A dry kibble diet is not healthy for your cat.
    • Do pet your cats. They need love and assurance too.
    • Do keep their litterboxes clean.
    • Do provide an extra pillow or foam because cats NEED to scratch.
    • Do let your cats spend their time outside even if it is just for a bit.

    What is Not Good for Cats?

    A list of some things we should not do to and with our cats.

    • Do not tolerate dirty litter boxes. These make your cats uncomfortable and might eliminate their waste elsewhere.
    • Do not rub their bellies, especially if they are wild cats.
      You CAN rub their bellies, but most cats don't like them. Or else they will scratch you.
    • Do not tolerate a dry kibble-only diet.
    • Do not eat any citrus near them because the smell is too strong for them.
    • Do not be too loud as cats hate loud and crowded places/people.
    • Do not bring them along car rides as they may have motion sickness. If ever, go to a vet and check if your cat has motion sickness.
    • Do not pet them aggressively.
    • Do not go to the vet and ask them to cut off all of your cat's nails. This affects your cat greatly.\
    • Do not spay your female cats without them experiencing birth beforehand.
