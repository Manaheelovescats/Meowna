    • Foods for Your Cat?

    Our cats are very comforting and they love to eat.
    As much as they love eating, like us, they too must stay healthy.
    Here are some lists of foods that they should and should not eat.

    Should Eat

    There are foods you may not know are healthy for your cats. There are also food they can eat. Here are some of them!

    • Steamed Sweet Potatoes
    • Cooked Egg Yolk/Yellow Part of the Egg or Scrambled Eggs
    • Wet Cat Food
    • Dry Kibble (but not too often, must feed wet food from time to time)
    • Cooked Beef, Chicken, or Turkey
    • Fish Oils
    • Cantaloupes
    • Oatmeal
    • Corn
    • Spinach
    • Pumpkin
    • Blueberries
    • Strawberries
    • Peas
    • Carrots
    • Apples
    • Broccoli
    • Cucumbers
    • Watermelons
    • Coconut Oil (not too much)
    • Cooked Fish

    Should NOT Eat

    As such, there are also foods they shouldn't eat. Here is a list of some of them.

    • Caffeine/Coffee
    • Garlic
    • Grapes
    • Raisins
    • Cheese
    • Onions
    • Raw Meat
    • Milk
    • Xylitol (sweetener)
    • Leeks
    • Spring Onions
    • Chocolate
    Reminder: Some foods aren't listed. These are just POSSIBLE foods you may try feeding your cat.
